Experiment Intro

Thank you for taking the time to help us with our experiment. During the experiment, you will be exposed to a series of images or sounds. For each file, you will be asked to submit you response on how you feel about the file. There are three responses:

  • Like
  • Neutral
  • Dislike

The response buttons will be enabled after 15 seconds of showing the file. This will allow us to capture EEG brainwave data about your feelings towards the image/sound.

Please relax and avoid any unnecessary movements while we calibrate the EEG device.
